WAƘA 4: "Dajjal a kan Kursiyi"



[Gabatarwa]

[Babi 1]
Obama a cikin inuwa yana jan kowane igiya,
Dajjal yana tasowa, suna naɗa shi sarki,
Matar shugaba Michael da kafaɗu masu faɗi sosai,
Kumburin cikin riga wanda suka yi ƙoƙarin ɓoye.

[Babi 2]
Big Mike yana rawa a shirin Ellen,
Joan Rivers ta faɗi gaskiya, sannan sai ta je,
"Dukanmu mun sani," ta ce tare da murmushi,
Ta mutu a kan tebur—haka suke nasara.

[Amshi]
Barry da Michael, ma'aurata masu ƙarfi daga jahannama,
Sun yaudari dukan al'umma da sihirin ƙarya,
Amma gaskiya tana ci gaba da yaɗuwa, muna gani bayan labulen,
Shirin Dajjal—ku duba yana gaza!

[Babi 3]
Yana ƙin duniya lebur, za ku iya gani a fuskarsa,
Yana tura ƙaryar duniya, yana tilasta sararin samaniya na ƙarya da ƙarfi,
Yana fushi sosai lokacin da batun ya taso,
A kan kariya kamar jahannama, ba zai iya barin ba.

[Babi 4]
Don me yake fusata idan wasa ne kawai?
Don me masu imani da duniya lebur suke damunsa?
Saboda dukan gidan katunan zai faɗi,
Lokacin da mutane suka sani suna tsaye a kan ƙasa mai ƙarfi.

[Amshi]

[Babi 5]
Michelle ta rubuta littafi game da "zama" wane?
Zama mace? Mun riga mun sani,
Ma'auratan Obama sun yaudari dukan al'umma,
Ruɗin Shaiɗan, abin ƙyama.

[Babi 6]
Barack yana nufin walƙiya, ku bincika a cikin rubutu,
"Na ga Shaiɗan ya faɗi," menene na gaba?
Luka goma sha takwas, Yesu ya faɗa a fili,
Walƙiya daga sama, wannan sunan Obama ne.

[Amshi]

[Gadar]
Mulki daga D.C., kursiyin dabba,
Shekaru bakwai na tsanani, daga yamma zuwa gabas,
Sarrafa hannun ƙarfe a kan kowace al'umma,
Alamar dabba, tilasta allurar rigakafi.
Ba za ku iya saya ko sayarwa ba tare da ɗinkin hannu ba,
Ko hatimin a goshi, wannan alamar Shaiɗan ce,
Shida shida shida, lambar mutum,
Wahayin Yohanna goma sha uku, duk bisa ga shiri.

[Ƙarshe]
Obama zai faɗi... lokacin da Uba ya kira,
An fallasa Michael... kowa ya sani.
