WAƘA 6: "Maganganun Maƙaryata"



[Gabatarwa]

[Babi 1]
Trump ya yi magana a fili idan kuna da kunnuwa don ji,
"Ina tashi kullum, ina da jirgi, a fili,
757, kyakkyawan jirgi, mafi kyau,
Da duniya ta kasance zagaye, da na sani—ba kamar sauran ba."

[Babi 2]
Buzz Aldrin ya zame, bai iya riƙe ƙarya ba,
"Ba mu je ba," kusan ya yi kuka,
"Haka abin ya faru, yana da kyau a san dalilin,"
Saukar wata na ƙarya, ba su taɓa taɓa sama ba.

[Amshi]
"Za mu san shirinmu na ƙaryar bayanai ya cika,
Lokacin da duk abin da jama'ar Amurka suka gaskata ƙarya ne,"
William Casey, daraktan CIA, ya gaya wa shugaba,
Kuma ga mu nan—al'umma ta imani na ƙarya!

[Babi 3]
Hillary Clinton ta ce ta karya rufin gilashi,
Miliyan goma sha takwas na fashe—ƙarya tana yaɗuwa,
Rufin gilashi ƙarya ne kamar komai,
'Yan tsana a kan igiyoyi suna rawa wa Shaiɗan.

[Babi 4]
Red Hot Chili Peppers sun saka gaskiya a cikin waƙa,
"Sarari an yi shi a cikin gindin Hollywood"—ku raira tare,
Californication ya gaya muku komai,
Amma kun yi gyaran kai, ba ku ji komai ba.

[Amshi]

[Babi 5]
"Da ni ne Shaiɗan," Paul Harvey ya fara,
"Da na haɗiye dukan duniya, wannan shine shirona,
Da na lalata coci-coci, na yi rada a kunne,
'Littafi Mai Tsarki tatsuniya ne,' da na ciyar da ƙarya shekaru."

[Babi 6]
"Da na sa iyalai su yi faɗa, na raba coci-coci,
Al'ummomi sun lalata kansu—da na jagoranci,
Alƙalai suna haɓaka ƙazanta, an kori Allah,
Daga kotunan da makarantu—kamar yadda aka annabta."

[Amshi]

[Gadar]
"Da na maye gurbin addini na gaskiya da ilimin halin ɗan adam,
In sa kimiyya a matsayin allah—abin da zan yi ne,
Da na jarabci firistoci zuwa lalata, kuɗi da yara maza,
In juya Ista zuwa ƙwai da Kirsimeti zuwa kayan wasa."

[Babi 7]
"Da na tuɓe ku a fili, in ja ku zuwa gado,
Da cututtuka marasa magani—kuma za ku mutu,
A wasu kalmomi idan ni ne Shaiɗan da na ci gaba—"
Abin da yake yi yanzu, lalata bil'adama!

[Ƙarshe]
Kalmomin kansu suna hukunta su, gaskiya a fili take,
Ku buɗe idanunku ku shiga haske.
