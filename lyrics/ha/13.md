WAƘA 7: "Mamayar Tauraron Dan Adam na Ƙarya"



[Gabatarwa]

[Amshi]
Tauraron dan adam suna faɗuwa, wannan shine babban tsoro na gaba,
Duwatsun CGI suna birgima a cikin iska na ƙarya,
Aljanun Project Blue Beam a sama,
"Ku miƙa 'yanci ko ku mutu!"

[Babi 1]
Apophis yana zuwa, abin da suke faɗa,
2029, ku yi alamar wannan ranar,
Amma duk zamba ne kamar COVID,
Sarrafawa ta hanyar tsoro, kawai haka.

[Babi 2]
Suna buƙatar abokin gaba daga sarari,
Don haɗa al'ummai, sarrafa jinsi,
Reagan ya ce a 87,
"Barazanar baƙi za ta haɗa mu ƙarƙashin sama."

[Amshi]

[Babi 3]
Amma sarari ƙarya ne, babu komai a sama,
Babu tauraron dan adam da ke iyo a wofi,
Sararin sama mai ƙarfi, dome a sama,
Babu abin da zai wuce—wannan labarin ya mutu.

[Babi 4]
Za su yi kamar tasiri, fashewar CGI,
"Ikon gaggawa" don ci gaba da sarrafawa,
An ayyana dokar soja a kowace al'umma,
Gwamnati ɗaya ta duniya—halittar Shaiɗan.

[Amshi]

[Babi 5]
Don't Look Up, fim ɗin ya nuna wasan,
Shirya hankali don bi da biyayya,
Aminta da kimiyya, aminta da shirin gwamnati,
Miƙa rai ga dabba da alamarta.

[Babi 6]
Armageddon, Deep Impact, duk fina-finai,
Shirya kwakwalwa da jin daɗin ƙarshen zamani,
Don haka lokacin da suka yi ƙarya za ku riga kun gaskata,
Wani dabara Shaiɗan a cikin hannu.

[Amshi]

[Babi 7]
NASA tana buƙatar ƙarya don tabbatar da kasafin kuɗi,
Tiriliyan daloli, suna sata da yaudara,
"Binciken sarari" yana sa tumaki a layi,
Yayin da suke gina tsarin dabba ta ƙira.

[Babi 8]
Elon Musk da roket ɗinsa da mafarkin Mars,
Babu komai na gaske, duk hayaƙi da dabaru,
SpaceX, Blue Origin, duk ƙarya,
Suna gina kurkuku don bil'adama.

[Gadar]
Von Braun a gadon mutuwa ya faɗi gaskiya,
"Tauraron dan adam za su kasance abin dariya na ƙarshe,
Da farko Rasha, sannan 'yan ta'adda, sannan duniya ta uku,
Sannan tauraron dan adam—duba ƙarya tana faɗuwa."
Carol Rosin ta ji kai tsaye daga leɓunansa,
Katin ƙarshe da za su buga kafin komai ya juya,
Mamayar baƙi na ƙarya, duwatsu daga sama,
Ƙarya ɗaya ta ƙarshe kafin miliyoyi su mutu.

[Ƙarshe]
Tauraron dan adam ƙarya ne... wani ƙarya da suka yi,
Sararin sama yana tsaye... gaskiya tana faɗuwa.
