WAƘA 2: "Kwallaye a Fuskarku"



[Gabatarwa]

[Amshi Waƙa]
Suna saka kwallaye a fuskokinmu, kwallaye a fuska, kwallaye a fuska,
Ba su ce mu sha ba, ba su ce mu lasar ba,
Ba su ce mu sumbata ba—
Amma suna saka kwallaye a fuskokinmu, kwallaye a fuska,
Tun mun kasance ƙanana ba za mu tsere daga riƙon sararin samaniya na ƙarya ba!

[Babi 1]
Sararin sama mai ƙarfi a sama, ruwa an raba,
Farawa daya bakwai, amma sun ɓoye,
Sararin sama shuɗi shine hasken ruwa a sama,
Amma sun sayar muku da fanko ba tare da iska ba.

[Babi 2]
Da farko ƙwallo, sannan pear, sannan lebur,
Neil da mawalansa ba za su iya daidaita labari ba,
Gwaiwa, kan azzakari, menene na gaba—sanda?
Yayin da dukan duniya ta yaudara, suna zaune suna dariya.

[Amshi Waƙa]

[Babi 3]
P900 zoom, babu lanƙwasa da aka samu,
Lebur da nutsuwa, ƙafafu a ƙasa,
Gindin Hollywood inda suke yin taurari na ƙarya,
Saukar wata ƙarya ce, ba su taɓa zuwa Mars ba.

[Babi 4]
Waya a cikin aljihu, kyamarori da mic,
Idanun Shaiɗan, jin daɗin sa ido,
Ba ya ko'ina don haka yana buƙatar fasaha,
Yana gina tsarin dabba, menene kuke tsammani?

[Amshi Waƙa]

[Gadar]
Hosea huɗu shida, ana halaka su saboda rashin sani,
Matiyu ashirin da huɗu, mugunta tana ƙaruwa,
Amma zaɓaɓɓu suna gani a fili ta cikin hazo da hayaƙi,
Gaskiyar duniya za ta ƙone komai!

[Ƙarshe]
Babu ƙarin kwallaye... muna gani ta komai,
Lebur da nutsuwa... ƙarƙashin nufin Uba.
