WAƘA 5: "Ƙaryar Siffar Pear"



[Gabatarwa]

[Amshi]
Siffar pear, oblate, ba za su iya daidaita ba,
Daga ƙwallo zuwa gwaiwa, yanzu suna muhawara,
Blue marble shine Photoshop—Robert Simmon ya yarda,
"Dole ne ta zama ƙarya," eh ya ce—an yi mana albarka!

[Babi 1]
Robert Simmon, Mista Blue Marble da kansa,
Ya saka gaskiya a kan sheli,
"Photoshop ne, amma dole ne ta kasance,"
Hotuna masu haɗuwa don ku da ni.

[Babi 2]
Ba hoto ba, ba a taɓa ɗauka ba,
An yaudari dukan duniya, amma a ƙarshe muna farka,
Kowace "hoto" na Duniya zane ne, rendering,
Ƙaryar CGI daga mai sayarwa na ƙarya.

[Amshi]

[Babi 3]
Neil deGrasse Tyson da juyin pear dinsa,
Kudu mai kauri—daga ina za mu fara?
Jiya ƙwallo, yau spheroid oblate,
Gobe pear—waɗannan maƙaryata fanko ne!

[Babi 4]
Babu daidaito, babu tabbataccen shaida,
Graphics na kwamfuta kawai suna fasa rufin,
Suna canza siffa lokacin da ya dace,
Ƙwallo, pear, ƙwai—duk 'ya'yan itace na ƙarya!

[Amshi]

[Babi 5]
Duniyar gwaiwa, abin da suka nuna da farko,
Yanzu siffar pear, wanne ya fi muni?
Na gaba za su ce muna rayuwa a kan sanda,
Sannan dukanmu—jira, kada ku yi dariya.

[Babi 6]
Saboda ba abin dariya ba ne lokacin da kuka ga wasan,
Kowace siffa da suke tura wani ɓangare ne na wannan wasan,
Shirin ɓoye abin da ke ƙarƙashin ƙafafunku,
Filin lebur na gaskiya da ba sa son ku sadu.

[Ƙarshe]
Duniya mai siffar pear... mun san darajarta,
Lebur da nutsuwa... ƙarƙashin nufin Uba.
